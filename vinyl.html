Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT)

Southend Flooring is a leading luxury vinyl tile flooring fitter in the Southend area. Ideal for both domestic and commercial flooring, we offer a fantastic range of LVT flooring at competitive prices.

Customers love LVT because of its durability, realistic finish, and easy maintenance. Our team can help you choose the right luxury vinyl flooring for your room, use case, and budget.

What is LVT flooring?

Luxury vinyl tile flooring, also known as LVT, is a hard-wearing and natural-looking flooring made from quality vinyl. Available in hundreds of beautiful wood, stone, and marble-inspired finishes, it makes a great alternative to traditional laminate or real wood flooring.

It looks incredibly authentic, but it’s also practical. LVT is easy to clean and maintain, making it a strong option for busy homes and commercial spaces alike.

Why choose luxury vinyl tiles?

Southend Flooring loves LVT because of its non-slip surface, making it a practical and safer option for both family homes and commercial spaces.

These tiles work particularly well in kitchens and bathrooms, where easy cleaning and spill resistance really matter. They also retain heat well and are compatible with underfloor heating, making them comfortable as well as durable.

We use trusted premium brands including Amtico, Karndean, and Polyflor, and our trained team carries out all sub-floor preparation in house.
