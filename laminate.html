How laminate flooring is made

Laminate flooring is made from several layers fused together under high pressure and high temperature. Each layer plays a role in durability, appearance, and stability.

Wear layer
The top surface you walk on, designed to be highly scratch-resistant and durable.
Design layer
The printed wood or patterned finish that gives laminate its visual style.
Core layer
The main structural board that provides strength, thickness, and stability.
Stabilising layer
The bottom balancing layer that helps support the flooring system overall.
