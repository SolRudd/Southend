Benefits of Amtico flooring

Amtico Flooring is a durable, low-maintenance alternative to real wood or stone flooring. Designed and manufactured in Coventry to superb quality, it is built to handle heavy daily use while still looking beautiful.

Hard-wearing finish
Built to withstand scratches, scuffs, spills, and everyday family life.
Long warranties
Ranges include strong residential warranties for long-term peace of mind.
Warm underfoot
Comfortable to live with and compatible with underfloor heating.
Water-resistant
Suitable for kitchens, bathrooms, and other practical spaces.
Easy to clean
Simple to wipe down and maintain without special treatment.
Premium appearance
Delivers the look of stone or timber without the same upkeep demands.
